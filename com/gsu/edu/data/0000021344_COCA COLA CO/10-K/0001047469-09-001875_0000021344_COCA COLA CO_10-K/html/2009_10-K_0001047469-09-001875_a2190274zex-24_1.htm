POWER OF ATTORNEY

 

KNOW ALL BY THESE PRESENTS THAT I, E. NEVILLE ISDELL, Chairman, Board of Directors of The Coca-Cola Company (the “Company”), do hereby appoint MUHTAR KENT, President and Chief Executive Officer and a Director of the Company, GARY P. FAYARD, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of the Company, GEOFFREY J. KELLY, Senior Vice President and General Counsel of the Company, CYNTHIA P. MCCAGUE, Senior Vice President of the Company, and CAROL C. HAYES, Associate General Counsel and Secretary of the Company, or any one of them, my true and lawful attorneys-in-fact for me and in my name for the purpose of executing on my behalf in any and all capacities the Company’s Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended December 31, 2008, or any amendment or supplement thereto, and causing such Annual Report or any such amendment or supplement to be filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission pursuant to the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended.

 

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand this 19th day of February, 2009.

 

 

 

/s/ E. Neville Isdell

 

 

Chairman, Board of Directors
