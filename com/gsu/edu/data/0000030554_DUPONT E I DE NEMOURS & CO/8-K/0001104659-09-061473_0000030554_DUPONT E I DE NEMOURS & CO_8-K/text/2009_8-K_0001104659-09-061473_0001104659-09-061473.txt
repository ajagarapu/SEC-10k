<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">DuPont&#146;s Board of Directors Appoints Ellen Kullman Chair</font></b></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;font-weight:bold;">Charles O. Holliday,&nbsp;Jr. to Retire as
Chair Dec.&nbsp;31</font></i></b></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WILMINGTON,
Del., Oct.&nbsp;30, 2009 &#150; DuPont today announced that its board of directors
has elected Chief Executive Officer Ellen Kullman, 53, Chair effective Dec.&nbsp;31.&#160; Charles&nbsp;O. Holliday,&nbsp;Jr., 61, Chair,
will retire from the board after 11<b>  </b>years as its Chairman.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">As
previously announced in September&nbsp;2008, Kullman became CEO effective Jan.&nbsp;1
after Holliday served as DuPont&#146;s CEO for 10 years. &#160;Holliday continued as Chair of DuPont and as a
member of the board for a brief transition until Kullman&#146;s expected succession
as Chair.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;I
am honored to have been selected Chair by the board of directors,&#148; Kullman
said. &#160;&#147;DuPont is well-positioned for our
next phase of delivering growth. &#160;We have
strong market positions and global channels to bring our culture of scientific
inquiry, innovation and strong customer focus to connect our technologies
directly with the needs of the global marketplace</font><i><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">.</font></i><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;Ellen&#146;s
innate leadership skills, acute market focus and strong track record were the
basis for the board&#146;s decision that she is the right person as its Chair. &#160;I am confident Ellen will be an outstanding Chair
and CEO,&#148; Holliday said. &#160;&#147;For over 35
years, I have had the unique privilege of being part of DuPont. &#160;I look forward to watching in the years ahead under
Ellen&#146;s leadership as DuPont continues to address the world&#146;s need for
science-based solutions.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">DuPont
is a science-based products and services company. Founded in 1802, DuPont puts
science to work by creating sustainable solutions essential to a better, safer,
healthier life for people everywhere. Operating in more than 70 countries,
DuPont offers a wide range of innovative products and services for markets
including agriculture and food; building and construction; communications; and
transportation.</font></p>
