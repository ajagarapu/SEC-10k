Item 5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders

On April 23, 2014, E. I. du Pont de Nemours and Company (the “company”) held its Annual Meeting of Stockholders. As of the close of business on February 26, 2014, the record date for the annual meeting, 916,275,513 shares of common stock, par value $.30 per share, of the company were outstanding and entitled to vote. A total of 766,807,675 shares of common stock were voted in person or by proxy, representing 83.69 percent of the shares entitled to be voted. The following are the final voting results on proposals considered and voted upon at the meeting, all of which are described in the company's 2014 Proxy Statement.
