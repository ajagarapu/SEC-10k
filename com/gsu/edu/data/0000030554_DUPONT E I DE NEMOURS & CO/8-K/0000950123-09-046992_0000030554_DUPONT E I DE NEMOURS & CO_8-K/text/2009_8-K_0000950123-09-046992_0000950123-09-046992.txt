<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">Richard R. Goodmanson will retire as Executive Vice President &#038; Chief Operating Officer of the
Company effective September&nbsp;30, 2009. To ensure his active participation on behalf of the Company
in ongoing litigation and other business matters, the Company has entered into a three-year
consulting agreement with Mr.&nbsp;Goodmanson, effective as of his retirement, pursuant to which he
shall be paid a $200,000 annual retainer plus a $2,000 per diem payment when actively involved in
litigation support and business projects on behalf of the Company. The agreement with Mr.
Goodmanson contains customary provisions, including a restriction on his ability to take on any
work that may create a conflict of interest, non-competition and non-solicitation covenants,
protection of confidential information and reimbursement of expenses associated with his
performance under the agreement.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">The foregoing description is qualified in its entirety by reference to the agreement, a copy of
