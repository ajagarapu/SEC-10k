<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Compensation Actions for Chair and Chief Executive Officer</font></u></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On January 23, 2012, the Board of Directors took the following actions with respect to Ellen J. Kullman, Chair of the Board and Chief Executive Officer:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Approved a 4.6% increase in Mrs. Kullman&#146;s annual salary from $1.339 million to $1.4 million, effective March 1, 2012;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Approved a short-term incentive payment for 2011 of $2.509 million under the Company&#146;s Equity and Incentive Plan (&#147;EIP&#148;);</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Established a target short-term incentive award for 2012 for Mrs. Kullman under the Company&#146;s EIP in the amount of $</font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;"> 2.253 million; and</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Approved a long-term incentive award (&#147;LTI Award&#148;) for Mrs. Kullman, effective February 6, 2012, under the Company&#146;s EIP. The award, which was approved at a value of $8.5 million, will be delivered in an equal mix, by fair value on the date of grant, of stock options, time-vested restricted stock units and performance-based restricted stock units.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Except with respect to change in control provisions, the terms of the LTI Award will be materially consistent with the award terms that are on file with the Securities and Exchange Commission (&#147;Commission&#148;) as Exhibit 10.8 to the Company&#146;s Form 10-Q Quarterly Report pursuant to Section 13 or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 for the quarterly period ended March 31, 2009.&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Section 7 of the EIP will govern treatment of the LTI Award in the event of a change in control. The Company&#146;s EIP is on file with the Commission as part of the Company&#146;s Definitive Proxy Statement pursuant to Section 14(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, which was filed on March 18, 2011.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Special Award for Chief Financial Officer</font></u></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On January 20, 2012, the Compensation Committee of the Company&#146;s Board of Directors approved a grant, effective February 6, 2012, under the Company&#146;s EIP of 50,000 time-vested restricted stock units (&#147;RSUs&#148;) to Nicholas C. Fanandakis, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer.&#160; The RSUs are designed to encourage the retention of this key executive by recognizing his strong current and future contributions to the Company.&#160; The RSUs will vest (assuming continued employment) as follows: (1) fifty percent (50%) of the units (including dividend equivalent units) will vest on February 6, 2014; and (2) fifty percent (50%) of the units (including dividend equivalent units) will vest on February 6, 2016.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">If the grantee&#146;s employment with the Company terminates due to total and permanent disability or death, all of the unvested RSUs will vest, provided the grantee has been employed by the Company for six months following the grant date.&#160; If the grantee&#146;s employment with the Company terminates for any other reason, including, but not limited to, resignation or retirement, prior to February 6, 2016, all of the unvested RSUs (including dividend equivalent units) will be forfeited.&#160; Section 7 of the EIP will govern treatment of the RSUs in the event of a change in control.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Except to the extent set forth above, the terms of the RSUs are materially consistent with the award terms on file with the Commission as Exhibit 10.8 to the Company&#146;s Form 10-Q Quarterly Report pursuant to Section 13 or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 for the quarterly period ended March 31, 2009.&#160; The Company&#146;s EIP is on file with the Commission as part of the Company&#146;s Definitive Proxy Statement pursuant to Section 14(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, which was filed on March 18, 2011.</font></p>
