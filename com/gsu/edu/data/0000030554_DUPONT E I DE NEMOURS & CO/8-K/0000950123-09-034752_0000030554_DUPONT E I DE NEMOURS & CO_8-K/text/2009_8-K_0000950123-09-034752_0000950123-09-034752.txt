<DIV align="center" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 18pt"><B>DuPont Takes Actions to Capitalize on Global Growth Opportunities</B><BR>
<B><I>Company Streamlines Organization and Names New Leadership Team</I></B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;WILMINGTON, Del., Aug. 13, 2009 &#151; DuPont CEO Ellen Kullman today announced actions supporting
the company&#146;s commitments to customers, sustainable growth, and delivering revenue and earnings
growth through market-driven science.
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The company will sharply focus its innovation engines on four emerging trends to meet the
increasing demand for food; protect people and the environment; decrease dependence on fossil
fuels; and capitalize on the growth of emerging markets. In addition, DuPont will strengthen its
alignment with customers and markets; drive disciplined scientific innovation directly from market
needs; increase transparency and accountability for results with a simplified organization; and
continue its intense focus on productivity. In support of the renewed focus, the company also
announced several leadership appointments.
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&#147;DuPont&#146;s leadership in market-driven science uniquely positions the company to capitalize on
four global trends that will define the coming decades. The actions we are taking today will give
our organization the focus and responsiveness it needs to deliver sustainable customer solutions to
the demands created by those trends,&#148; Kullman said.
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&#147;The people of DuPont successfully managed through the extraordinary economic conditions of
the past year and delivered on our commitments for the first half of 2009,&#148; Kullman added. &#147;DuPont
will meet the economic recovery as a stronger, faster and more agile global competitor. We will
accomplish this by driving responsibility and accountability ever-closer to our customers and
regions, providing greater transparency to our progress and results, streamlining our organization,
and maintaining an intense focus on productivity.&#148;
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><B>Streamlined Organization</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Key components of DuPont&#146;s more streamlined organization include:

associated with the platforms that house DuPont&#146;s businesses;</TD>
</TR>

</TABLE>

<DIV style="margin-top: 6pt">
<TABLE width="100%" border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" style="font-size: 10pt">
