<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On January&nbsp;9, 2011, E. I. du Pont de Nemours and Company (&#147;DuPont&#148;) announced that DuPont and its wholly-owned subsidiary, DuPont Denmark Holding ApS (the &#147;Acquisition Subsidiary&#148;), entered into a definitive agreement with Danisco A/S (&#147;Danisco&#148;), for the Acquisition Subsidiary to make a public tender offer for all of Danisco&#146;s outstanding shares at a price of DKK 665 in cash per share.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On April&nbsp;29, 2011, DuPont, through the Acquisition Subsidiary, announced that it made the following changes to the public tender offer:</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 110.7pt;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">it increased the price of its tender offer to acquire all of the outstanding shares of Danisco to DKK 700 in cash per share;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 110.7pt;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">the tender offer period has been extended to 11&nbsp;p.m. CEST (5&nbsp;p.m. EDT) on May&nbsp;13, 2011; and</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt 110.7pt;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="2" face="Symbol" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#183;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">it has exercised its right to reduce the minimum number of shares required to be tendered to 80% from 90% in order to close the tender offer.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:1.0in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">All other terms and conditions of the public tender offer remain unchanged.
