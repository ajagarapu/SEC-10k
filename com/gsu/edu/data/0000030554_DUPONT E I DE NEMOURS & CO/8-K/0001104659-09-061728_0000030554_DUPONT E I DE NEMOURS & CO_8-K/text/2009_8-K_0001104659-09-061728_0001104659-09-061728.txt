<p align="right" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Exhibit&nbsp;99.1</font></b></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">TEXT OF THE
AMENDMENT TO BYLAWS</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">OF</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">E. I. DU PONT DE
NEMOURS AND COMPANY</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Effective November&nbsp;1,
2009</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ARTICLE
V.</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">OFFICERS</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">SECTION&nbsp;1.&#160; Officers.&#160;
The Board shall appoint at least the following officers of the Company:
a Chair of the Board, <strike>an Executive Vice President DuPont-Finance</strike> a Chief
Executive Officer, one or more Executive Vice Presidents, <u>a Chief Financial
Officer</u>, a Treasurer and a Secretary.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The
Board and the Office of the Chief Executive, may appoint such other officers as
they deem necessary, who shall have such authority and shall perform such
duties as may be prescribed, respectively, by the Board or the Office of the
Chief Executive.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">SECTION&nbsp;6.&#160; <strike>Executive Vice President DuPont-Finance</strike>
<u>Chief Financial Officer</u>.&#160; The <strike>Executive
Vice President DuPont-Finance </strike><u>Chief Financial Officer</u> shall be the <strike>chief</strike>
<u>principal</u> financial officer of the Company and shall have such powers
and perform such duties as may be assigned to such <strike>Executive Vice President
DuPont-Finance</strike>  <u>Chief Financial Officer</u> by the Board or the Office of
the Chief Executive.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">SECTION&nbsp;7.&#160; Treasurer.&#160;
Under the general direction of the <strike>Executive Vice President
DuPont-Finance</strike>  <u>Chief Financial Officer</u>, the Treasurer shall have
such powers and perform such duties as may be assigned to such Treasurer by the
Board or the Office of the Chief Executive.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">SECTION&nbsp;9.&#160; Controller.&#160;
The Board may appoint a Controller.&#160;
Under the general direction of the <strike>Executive Vice President
DuPont-Finance</strike>  <u>Chief Financial Officer</u>, the Controller shall have such
powers and perform such duties as may be assigned to such Controller by the
Board or the Office of the Chief Executive.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">ARTICLE
VI.</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">MISCELLANEOUS</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">SECTION&nbsp;6.&#160; Corporate Seal.&#160; The seal of the Company shall be circular in
form, containing the words &#147;E. I. DU PONT DE NEMOURS AND CO.&#148; and &#147;DELAWARE&#148; on
the circumference, surrounding the words &#147;FOUNDED&#148; and &#147;SEAL,&#148; and the date &#147;1802.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The
seal shall be in the custody of the Secretary.&#160;
A duplicate of the seal may be kept and used by the <strike>Executive Vice President
DuPont-Finance</strike>  <u>Chief Financial Officer</u>, any Vice President &#151; DuPont
Finance, the Treasurer, or by any Assistant Secretary or Assistant Treasurer.</font></p>
