Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure

On April 23, 2015, The Chemours Company (“Chemours”), a wholly-owned subsidiary of E. I. du Pont de Nemours and Company, issued a press release announcing the offering of debt securities under Rule 144A and Regulation S under the Securities Act of 1933, subject to market and other conditions, of approximately $2,125 million aggregate principal amount of senior unsecured dollar-denominated notes and €350 million aggregate principal amount of senior unsecured Euro-denominated notes.

A copy of the Chemours press release is furnished herewith as Exhibit 99.1. The information contained in Item 7.01, including Exhibit 99.1, of this report on Form 8-K shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liability of that section, and it will not be incorporated by reference into any registration statement or other document filed by the Registrant under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act except as expressly set forth by specific reference in such a filing.
