<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><U><B>Net Income and Global Consolidated Sales </B></U>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Net income attributable to DuPont for the second quarter 2009 was $417&nbsp;million versus $1,078
million in the prior year. The decline in net income principally reflects significantly lower sales
volume, current quarter restructuring charges, and adverse currency impact. Consolidated net sales
in the second quarter of $6.9&nbsp;billion were 22&nbsp;percent lower than prior year, principally reflecting
19&nbsp;percent lower volume and a net 1&nbsp;percent reduction due to portfolio changes. Local prices were
3&nbsp;percent higher, largely driven by higher seed prices, but were more than offset by a 5&nbsp;percent
negative impact from currency exchange rates. Lower sales volume reflects the recessionary impact
across global markets served by DuPont. The table below shows sales by region and variances versus
